About Our Company

Located in Miami, FL, Electrostatic Industrial Painting Inc. is a leading provider of industrial coating solutions. With our state-of-the-art facility at 6801 NW 25th Ave, we specialize in electrostatic painting, plating, and comprehensive surface treatment services. Our expertise extends across industrial paint coating, electro coating, dipping services, wrapping services, and specialized machining processes.

We pride ourselves on delivering superior finishing solutions for manufacturing and industrial applications, combining cutting-edge technology with expert craftsmanship. Our services include welding, brazing, soldering, and advanced machining processes, all designed to meet the highest industry standards.
