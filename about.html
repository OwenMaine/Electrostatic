About Electrostatic Industrial Painting Inc

Your Premier Industrial Coating Solutions Provider Since 1990

Our Legacy

Based in Miami, Florida, Electrostatic Industrial Painting Inc has established itself as a leading provider of industrial coating solutions. From our state-of-the-art facility at 6801 NW 25th Ave, we serve manufacturers and industrial clients across the region with cutting-edge surface treatment technologies.

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering superior quality finishes while maintaining the highest standards of environmental responsibility and workplace safety. Our team of certified technicians combines decades of experience with modern coating technologies to ensure exceptional results for every project.
